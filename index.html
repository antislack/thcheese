le grand frommage

Our signature item is the Medicated Camembert cheese. Handcrafted in small batches from locally sourced organic foods, this cheese was born and aged in the San Francisco / Bay Area of California.

Medicated with THC

The Medicated Camembert is a low dose (0.0x% THC per serving) true-to-form cheese developed by Hervè Mons alumni and head cheesemaker, Juliana Clark.

a rined delicacy

THCHEESE's Medicated Camembert is a rined cow's milk cheese made to embody the spirit of Normandy.

Tasting Notes

Our camembert is a light funky, herbaceous and mushroomy, bloomy, rined cheese, that strikes a delicate rich and buttery finish.
