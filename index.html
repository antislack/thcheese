Frequently Asked Questions

We are so happy that you have chosen to enjoy our products. In order to ensure that your edible experience is a positive one we have taken the liberty of answering some frequently asked questions below. It is important to note that this page is just to provide information about THCheese products and none of the information here should be listed as legal or medical advice. It is highly recommended that you speak to your doctor for your own personal recommended dosage.

How strong are THCheese products?

The milligram dosage is listed on the outside of every one of our packages. We created our goods to be low dose so that you can enjoy your cheese, charcuterie, or pickles with any meal in normal portions. Most of our serving suggestions are based off a 20mg THC dose. This dosage is good for experienced users. For first time users we suggest a 5mg dose, and occasional users should do fine with 10mg.

Where can I find THCheese products?

Ask your local CA dispensary to start carrying our products and we will be on those shelves soon! We do not ship out of state due to federal regulation.

How do I find out more about ordering your products for my establishment?

Use the contact us feature on this site and we will return your query within 3 business days.

About Us

THCheese is dedicated to making quality small batch edibles. Three friends with a common goal of elevating the edible culture who believe in the concepts of sustainable farming and responsible dining. We are a Chef, a Cheesemaker, and a Cheesemonger with a passion for fine foods. We will never stop making meals, innovating recipes, and pursuing a high standard for our patients and our community. Our cannabis farms are just as important as our dairy farms and we are excited to see where our craft cannabis culture can take us.


We make low dose high end edibles so that you can consume an entire serving of our product and enjoy a mild, mellow, controllable experience. We love cannabis and we focus on the food. Quality is important both in our medicine and our ingredients.
